Mitten Candle Koozies

With an old pair of mittens, you can make a stylish koozie for all you candles at home.

Instructions

  1. Cut cuff off each mitten just below thumb. Fold raw edge of cuff portion under and glue to make a hem.
  2. For button flowers: Glue a bead to center of each button. Glue buttons to center of cuff in a cluster. For leaf, cut 1â€³ off thumb. Pinch to make a small pleat and glue onto middle of cuff.
  3. For trim: Glue a length of decorative trim along hemmed edge of cuff.
  4. Slip cuff over votive holder. Place candle inside.
